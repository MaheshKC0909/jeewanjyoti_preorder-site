## System Overview

JeewanJyoti Care is a comprehensive digital healthcare platform that provides:
- **Health Monitoring**: Real-time tracking of vital signs and health metrics
- **Telemedicine**: Virtual consultations with healthcare professionals
- **Appointment Management**: Schedule and manage medical appointments
- **Health Data Visualization**: Interactive charts and graphs for health trends
- **Secure Communication**: Chat with doctors and healthcare providers
- **Multi-user Support**: Connect with family members and caregivers

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React.js with Vite
- **UI Framework**: Tailwind CSS with Lucide React icons
- **Authentication**: Firebase Authentication
- **Backend API**: RESTful API at `https://jeewanjyoti-backend.smart.org.np`
- **Charts**: Recharts for data visualization
- **Payment**: Khalti payment integration

## User Registration and Login

### Registration Process

#### Individual Users
1. Click **Register** on the landing page
2. Select **Individual Account**
3. Fill in the required information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password
   - Confirm Password
4. Complete the profile information form
5. Verify your email address
6. Set up your health profile

#### Institutional Users
1. Click **Register** on the landing page
2. Select **Institutional Account**
3. Provide institutional details:
   - Institution Name
   - Registration Number
   - Contact Person Details
   - Official Email
   - Password
4. Upload necessary documents
5. Wait for admin approval

### Login Options

#### Standard Login
1. Enter your email/username
2. Enter your password
3. Select user type (Individual/Institutional)
4. Click **Login**

#### Google Sign-In
1. Click the **Google Sign-In** button (blue "G" button)
2. Complete Google authentication in the popup
3. Grant necessary permissions
4. You'll be redirected to the dashboard

### Password Recovery
1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account Management
**Q: How do I change my email address?**
A: Go to Settings > Account Settings > Update Email. You'll need to verify the new email address.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Each person should have only one account. Use User Mapping to connect with family members.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support or go to Settings > Account Settings > Delete Account. Note that this action is irreversible.

### Health Data
**Q: How accurate is the health monitoring?**
A: The platform uses FDA-approved algorithms and devices. However, always consult healthcare providers for medical decisions.

**Q: Can I export my health data?**
A: Yes, go to Profile > Medical Documents > Export Data to download your complete health history.

**Q: How often is data synchronized?**
A: Real-time synchronization for connected devices. Manual refresh available for imported data.

### Appointments
**Q: How far in advance can I book appointments?**
A: You can book appointments up to 30 days in advance, depending on doctor availability.

**Q: What if I need to cancel an appointment?**
A: You can cancel up to 24 hours before the appointment time. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

**Q: Can I get a refund for a cancelled appointment?**
A: Refunds are processed according to the doctor's cancellation policy. Contact support for assistance.

### Technical Issues
**Q: Why is the platform running slowly?**
A: Check your internet connection, clear browser cache, or try using a different browser.

**Q: I can't see my health data charts.**
A: Ensure JavaScript is enabled and your browser is updated to the latest version.
